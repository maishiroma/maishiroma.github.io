About Me

Hello! I recently graduated from Chapman University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Game Development and Programming. I am involved in numerous projects both in and out of the classroom, ranging from one off game jams to viable submissions to student game showcases. My diligence for work and passion in video games led me to pursue a career path of learning the science behind them. Whenever I get the chance, I strive to learn new knowledge that will help me in future programming endeavors or offer assistance in outside projects. During my free time, I catch up on my exponential list of games, network with people of similar interests, and play the Japanese Taiko drums.
